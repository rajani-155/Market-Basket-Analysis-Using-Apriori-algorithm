Parallel Plot

This parallel plot between the antecedent and consequent items shows associations and dependencies of items within the dataset.

The antecedent items are placed on the left side of the plot, while the consequent item is positioned on the right side. The position of each line on the vertical axes indicates the presence or absence of a particular item within the antecedent or consequent of the rule. If an item is present, the line intersects the corresponding axis; if an item is absent, the line does not intersect the axis. The shape and pattern of the lines across the axes provide insights into the combinations and relationships between items.
