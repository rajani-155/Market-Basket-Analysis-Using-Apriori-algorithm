Scatterplot

In this scatter plot, the x-axis represents the support value and y-axis represents the confidence value of the rules generated by the Apriori algorithm.

Each point on the scatter plot represents a rule generated by the Apriori algorithm, with its position determined by the support and confidence values of that rule. A higher support value corresponds to a point further along the x-axis, indicating that the rule occurs more frequently. Similarly, a higher confidence value corresponds to a point further along the y-axis, indicating a stronger relationship between the antecedent (the items on the left-hand side of the rule) and the consequent (the item on the right-hand side of the rule).

Clusters or groupings of points suggest similar support and confidence values, indicating rules with similar characteristics. Outliers, points that deviate significantly from the main cluster, may represent rules with exceptional support and confidence values, which could be of particular interest or importance.
